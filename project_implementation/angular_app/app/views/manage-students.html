Welcome to Schoolbloc!

Add students by providing their school id and access token provided to you via email.

Once provided, you may manage each of your students' courses by selecting a student.

Choose 'Manage classes' from the menu to view the selected student's required coures, as well as adding and prioritizing the student's elective courses.

To log out, click on your username, and 'Logout'.

Please note, you will be unable to view your students' schedule from this website. The school will provide you with your students' schedule at a later time.
